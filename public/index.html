What do I want to do?

I get asked this a lot. For now, I am content to learn from others with more skill than me in code. I lean toward front-end, where my creative abilities can shine, but I am not against learning anything and everything. Eventually, I see myself entering a position of leadership or in some kind of educational capacity, whether it's training juniors or teaching newbies about this field. I am still young and getting the feel of the tech field. I have plenty of time to find the right niche for myself and I am happy to hear about what kinds of positions you can offer to someone with my skills.
